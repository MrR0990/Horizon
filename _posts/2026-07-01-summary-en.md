The essay argues these materials are not fully natural nor artificial, creating a new category of 'strange rocks'.

  > This analysis examines how weight compression (8-bit and 4-bit quantization) affects performance and sparse autoencoder (SAE) applicability on Google DeepMind's Gemma 3 4B and 12B models, finding that performance degrades only modestly at 4-bit while SAE reconstruction ability remains largely intact.
